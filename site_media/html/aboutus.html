What is Stud2.0

Stud2.0 is all about connecting students and industry in open ways

Open Ways ? How ?

Lets Suppose:

  • If a college student can host his class-room assignments online in one single place in an easy way or share it with other class mates or anybody across the globe how would it be ?
  • If a college student can openly raise a question about some topic in his academic syllabus, which he/she is facing a difficulty in understanding with and if Industry Experts or even his Senior can help online, how would it be ?
  • If a Company HR or any recruiter has a multi-dimensional view of any student academic career, when compared to a typical 2-page word document, how better it would be ?

Stud2.0 is all about trying to solve these problems
